**Update Airport**
----
  Update an airport

* **URL**

  /rs/airport/:id

* **Method:**

  `PUT`
  
*  **URL Params**

   **Required:** `id=[integer]`

* **Data Params**

  **Required:** `airportcode=[string]`
    
  **Required:** `city=[string]`
    
  **Required:** `country=[string]`
    
  **Required:** `name=[string]`

* **Success Response:**

  * **Code:** 200
